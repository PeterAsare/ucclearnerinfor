Program Categories

Our department offers comprehensive programs designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills in mathematics and ICT education. Whether you're interested in pure mathematics or mathematics education, we have programs tailored to your career aspirations.

Learn More
BSC Mathematics

Our Bachelor of Science in Mathematics program provides a strong foundation in mathematical theory and applications, preparing students for careers in research, finance, data science, and more.

BED Mathematics

The Bachelor of Education in Mathematics program combines mathematical content with pedagogical training, preparing graduates to teach mathematics at the secondary school level.
